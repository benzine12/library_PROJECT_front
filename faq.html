Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Book CRM is a comprehensive library management system that allows you to manage books, customers, loans, and reports efficiently.

You can add a new book or customer using the forms available in the respective sections. Simply navigate to the 'Books' or 'Customers' page, click the 'Add' button, and fill out the required information.

The loan system allows you to loan out books to customers. When adding a loan, you can select the book and customer, specify the loan date, and track the return date. The system also tracks overdue loans and sends notifications for books that need to be returned.

Reports can be generated in the 'Reports' section. You can view detailed information on loans, customer activity, book availability, and other metrics to help you manage your library.
